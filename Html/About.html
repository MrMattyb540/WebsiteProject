About Us





At Matthew's Car Maintenance, we specialise in making sure that repairs are done with care and finished at a professional level. Our Business started back in 2020, during lockdown when there was nothing else to do. Instead, we proritised our time by fixing and restoring cars back to their former glory. Doing so, allowed us to bring in some money and eventually start our business. We started recieving loads of orders and appointments, and because of this we needed to have our own website which would allow us to take bookings and start our very own community.
